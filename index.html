1. Introduction

Welcome to Otzoo — a Real-Time Local Truth Platform that connects users with local shops, services, home help, and properties in their area.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Android application. By using Otzoo, you agree to this policy.

🔒 We are committed to protecting your privacy. Your data is never sold to third parties.

📋

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our services:

  • Full name and email address (via Google Sign-In)
  • Phone number (for business registration)
  • City and location data (for nearby search features)
  • Device information (for app optimization)
  • Business listing information (shop/service details you provide)
  • Usage data (app interactions, features used)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Razorpay — we do not store card details)
⚙️

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your Otzoo account
  • Show relevant local shops and services near you
  • Enable business owners to list their shops/services
  • Send notifications about shops you follow
  • Process subscription payments (Basic/Pro plans)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Show relevant advertisements via Google AdMob
📍

4. Location Data

Otzoo uses your location to show nearby shops, services, and properties. Location access is required for the "Nearby" feature to work properly.

  • Location is only accessed when you use the app
  • We do not track your location in the background
  • You can deny location permission — basic features will still work
  • Location data is used only for local search results
🔐

5. Google Sign-In & Third-Party Services

Otzoo uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In — for secure authentication
  • Supabase — for database and backend services
  • Razorpay — for payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements
  • Firebase — for push notifications

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

🗄️

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers with industry-standard encryption. We implement the following security measures:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure authentication via Google OAuth 2.0
  • Row-level security in our database
  • Payment data handled by Razorpay (we never store card details)
  • Regular security audits and updates
🤝

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only in these cases:

  • With service providers who help us operate the app (listed above)
  • When required by law or legal proceedings
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users
  • Business listing information is publicly visible to other users (as intended)
⚖️

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access your personal data at any time
  • Update or correct your profile information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt-out of marketing notifications
  • Request a copy of your data

📧 To exercise any of these rights, contact us at otzooofficial@gmail.com

📢

9. Advertisements

Otzoo displays advertisements powered by Google AdMob. These ads may be personalized based on your interests. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad targeting.

  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
  • We comply with Google AdMob policies
  • Ad revenue helps us keep the app free for users
👶

10. Children's Privacy

Otzoo is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

🔄

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of Otzoo after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
